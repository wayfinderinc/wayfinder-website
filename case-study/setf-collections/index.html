Southeast Toyota Finance

We collaborated with Southeast Toyota Finance (SETF) to create a digital solution to improve its collections experience on their website.

Case Study—UX/UI Design, Content Strategy, Information Architecture

The Brief

SETF offers financial services to nearly 700,000 retail and lease customers across Southeast Toyota's 177 dealers in Alabama, Florida, Georgia, North Carolina, and South Carolina.

Its website, SETF.com, is intended for registered customers to manage their accounts and vehicles, make payments, explore offers and coupons, and get help.

Unfortunately, some of their customers experience financial hardship and fall behind on their required monthly paments which can lead into collections. Their traditional method to raise awareness and help the customer is not effective.

The team that recently inherited the website saw an opportunity to improve its collections experience and position the brand as a hero.

Goals

SETF asked Wayfinder to address their challenge by revamping how they interact with their customers in collections through a personalized engagment strategy that will reduce the number of past due accounts.

The proposed digital solution will:

Increase

response rate to delinquent account notifications.

Improve

resolution of accounts over 60 days past due.

Collect

customer data to inform the future collections strategy.

Insights

  • 3+ days past due to start standard mail notification (varies based on risk level).
  • 60% of customers with past due accounts who continue to log in via setf.com.
  • Not all customers understand the impact of going into collections.
  • Not all customers understand the steps they need to take to avoid collections.
  • 75+ days past due to start repossession procedures.

Challenges and Recommendations

Traditional collection methods result in a negative experience. The conversation rates from phone calls and mailed letters are extreamly low. If 60% of customers who are past due continue to log into their accounts we should use that digital opportunity to engage customers and raise awareness.

The current methods available to resolve these issues are not optimal. Traditional collections phone calls and letters, although rational, can by dry. By humanizing the language used to communicate to customers in default we can create trust and drive them to respond to past due notifications.

We don't know why a customers account has gone into default. By creating a customer centric, personalized and guided experience, we can drive higher engagement and deliver messages that most deeply sympathize with each one of them. Additionally, we can gather data to understand their situation and assist the collections department with an outreach strategy for each unique case.

UX Strategy

We considered the ecosystem and there are two sides of the coin. On one side we have the customer with a financial struggle. On the other end we have SETF with a business goal to provide a solution that will benifit both its customers in default and its organization.

  • Customer - Digital and personalized experience to drive engagement and empower customers to resolve their debt.
  • SETF - Data-driven collections process will enable a personalized outreach strategy and improve recovery rates

Solution

Create a roadblock for customers in default who log into the website and send a humanized message during this critical moment that will drive them to engage and begin their recovery process.

SETF will use this moment to build empathy and understanding with the customer. Educate that SETF is here to help them avoid being sent to collections, or worst, having their vehicle repossessed.

The engagement will allow customers to self identify common life changes that can inform the collections team what is happening and allow them to provide a solution.

Architecture

Below is a visual reprentation of the structure and flow of the digital solution starting with the account roadblock.

Design

Unique experiences capture attention. Attention leads to emotional engagement. Emotional engagement builds memories. Memories influence decision making.

When a customer who is in default logs into their account they will be presented with an overlay. An experience that shows empathy and achieves that by utilizing the right tone and voice. Positioning the brand as a hero.

Account Roadblock

The left column of the experience raises awareness. Showcases vehicle details and amount due with an option to make a payment or contact through secure message or phone call.

The right column has humanized messaging that most deeply resonates with the customer. Empowering them to resolve their debt and keep them in the driver seat.

This personalized experience, at scale, connects with more customers and guides them through the collections process.

Question 1

With Wayfinder's online research and collaboration with the SETF collections team we identified the top 4 reasons a customer might fall behind on payments.

A customer has lost their job, has a lower income, death in the family or can have medical issues. If a customer does not fall into any of these options they can choose "other" and type a reason why.

Question 2

Once a customer has chosen a reason we ask a secondary question to gain deeper insight on their situation.

Contact

After completing the questionnaire the customer will choose to get help through a phone call or secure message. Allowing the SETF team to contact the customer on their time within a 24 time frame.

Outreach Strategy

All values are captured from the engagment and formatted into an email. The collected data insight from each customer interaction will inform SETF's outreach strategy. Providing the customer a personalized solution to resolve their debt.

Design for All Devices

Responsive or adaptive design are no longer the exception, but the rule. Considering our understanding of the online audience, their goals for accessing the website, the types of interactions, and our increasing reliance on mobile devices, we designed the experience to look and work equally well across devices while minimizing the differences in components and layouts to simplify the build.

Impact

Planting a positive experience blooms positive results. Within the first 30 days, SETF received over 1500 cases of customers motivated to be helped through their financial struggle. This has brought their previous default rate from 3.5%-5% to 2%-3.5%.

This personalized solution will have a future affect in improved payment behavior, reduced rates of future delinquency, higher customer retention and brand loyalty.
